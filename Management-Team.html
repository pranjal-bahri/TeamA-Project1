Party on a Plate - Meet the Team

Good food. Good company. Great parties.

Abby Ledger - Founder & CEO

Abby founded Party on a Plate in 2012 to bring crowd-pleasing comfort foods to every event. She is the mastermind behind the company's signature dishes. Abby leads the company's vision and loves taste-testing new recipes to keep every plate perfect.

Taylor Reed - Director of Operations

Taylor manages event logistics and staff scheduling to make sure every order arrives fresh and on time. He keeps events running smoothly behind the scenes with expert problem-solving and organization. Taylor ensures that every customer has a stress-free, seamless catering experience.

Jordan Price - Marketing Manager

Jordan oversees marketing, social media, and community partnerships for Party on a Plate. She connects the brand with schools, businesses, and local events. Jordan ensures all promotions are fun, engaging, and true to the company's personality.

Malik Johnson - Finance & Purchasing Officer

Malik manages budgeting, supplier contracts, and purchasing to keep the business efficient and sustainable. He ensures high-quality ingredients are always available without overspending. Malik's work behind the scenes ensures that every event runs smoothly and successfully.

Our Values:

  • Quality: Every plate is made with care and a sprinkle of love.
  • Community: Proudly feeding West Lafayette one party at a time.
  • Fun: Life's too short for boring food.

Team Goals:

  1. Deliver an amazing catering experience every time.
  2. Expand to more Indiana towns and college events.
  3. Keep innovating with fun new dishes and desserts.
